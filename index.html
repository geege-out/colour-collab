The Colour Collaborative is more than the name implies. We are a colourful bunch combining our varying talents and attributes in a collaboration celebrating creativity and the arts. Sharing our love of new design, innovative thinking, experimental undertakings and classic adaptations, we’re here to bring your home, office, and every space between to life with one-of-a-kind and original pieces.

We partner with artists showcasing emerging talent of refined craft, making art and artwork accessible to all.

All works are hand-made and all “treasures” are found locally and refurbished by hand.
